About Me

Hi, I'm Mekides! I'm a rising junior at the University of Pennsylvania, studying Computational Cognitive Science and minoring in Linguistics. I'm really interested in how language and technology work together, especially in Natural Language Processing (NLP).

Right now, I'm doing research in NLP with Professor Chris Callison-Burch at Penn. I'm learning how computers can understand and create human language, which connects to my interests in how the mind works, how language works, and how smart machines are built.
